This document lists allowed and prohibited language for the Results section.

SECTION 3.1 — TRANSITION GEOMETRY
================================================================================

ALLOWED PHRASES:
  - "exhibits a phase transition"
  - "order parameter R increases with K"
  - "disordered phase at low K; synchronized phase at high K"
  - "R transitions from approximately 0.08 to approximately 0.98"
  - "transition observed in the range K = 0 to 6"
  - "steepest change in R occurs near K approximately 2"
  - "consistent with Kuramoto synchronization literature"
  - "order parameter is the sole observable"

PROHIBITED PHRASES:
  - "universal critical point"
  - "precisely characterized Kc"
  - "order parameter discontinuity"
  - "first-order transition confirmed"
  - "transition verified across all topologies"
  - "paradigm shift"
  - "breakthrough"
  - "unprecedented"
  - "definitive evidence"
  - "proof of"

REVIEWER-TRIGGER WORDS:
  - "proof" -> use "evidence" or "observation"
  - "demonstrate" -> use "show" or "observe"
  - "establish" -> use "find" or "suggest"
  - "confirm" -> use "observe" or "measure"
  - "critical point" -> use "critical coupling" or "critical region"
