================================================================================
PER-ESTIMATOR LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

ORDER PARAMETER R:
  - Single scalar; no phase information
  - No verification with alternative order parameters
  - Computed as mean of last window (no temporal averaging optimization)

SUSCEPTIBILITY chi = N * Var(R_trials):
  - Variance of trial means (not temporal variance)
  - 4 trials only; chi estimate has high variance
  - chi ~ N^gamma fit from 3 points (low statistical power)
  - gamma not universal; varies 0.62 to 1.98

BINDER CUMULANT U4 = 1 - <R^4> / (3 <R^2>^2):
  - Requires 4 trials minimum for meaningful moments
  - Sensitive to simulation length (STEPS, TRANSIENT)
  - No standardized measurement protocol
  - Conflicting results between different simulation parameters

RELAXATION TIME tau:
  - 90% threshold is arbitrary
  - STEPS = 1000 insufficient for equilibration at some K values
  - 50-step stability criterion too strict; causes saturation
  - No autocorrelation-based tau measurement attempted

LOOP AREA (hysteresis):
  - Threshold 0.15 is arbitrary
  - Coupled forward/backward initialization (lower bound)
  - 4-trial average; single-trial variance not reported

================================================================================
PER-SCALING-RESULT LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

FINITE-SIZE SHARPENING:
  - Qualitative only; no quantitative width measure
  - No formal finite-size scaling analysis

CHI PEAK SCALING chi ~ N^gamma:
  - Gamma approximately 1.1 for A2A + Gaussian (not universal)
  - 3-point fit; no error bars; R2 inflated by low N

DATA COLLAPSE:
  - Kc fixed at 1.33 (not estimated)
  - nu fixed at 2.0 (assumed from literature)
  - No optimization; no collapse quality metric

KC APPROXIMATION:
  - Kc approximately 1.3 to 1.8 depending on estimator and N
  - No converged thermodynamic-limit estimate
  - Three-estimator convergence failed
