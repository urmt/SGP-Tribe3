REVIEWER LIMITATIONS PARAGRAPH
==============================

LIMITATIONS:

1. Dataset: Analysis restricted to CHB-MIT dataset (4 subjects from Phase 112 
   acquisition). Generalizability to other EEG datasets is unknown.

2. Observable selection: Results constrained to 10 specific metrics. Two 
   metrics (propagation asymmetry, graph entropy) showed preservation under 
   reduced-order models - the dependency is not universal across all possible 
   observables.

3. Survival threshold: The 15% survival criterion was specified a priori but 
   is not theoretically grounded. Sensitivity to threshold choice is not tested.

4. Preprocessing: FFT-based phase computation, 10% burst threshold, and 
   weighted adjacency matrices (no thresholding) were held constant. Effects 
   of alternative preprocessing choices are unknown.

5. Temporal window: 512-sample windows with 10% burst threshold may not capture 
   all relevant temporal scales.

6. Causal mechanism: The five factors are identified but their dynamical 
   interaction mechanism remains unknown. Results support WHAT is required, 
   not HOW the requirement emerges.

7. Governance corrections: Initial analyses had incomplete search (Phase 191) 
   and inconsistent verdict language (Phase 194), which were corrected in 
   subsequent governance phases.
