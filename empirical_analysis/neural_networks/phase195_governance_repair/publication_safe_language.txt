PUBLICATION-SAFE LANGUAGE
==========================

APPROVED WORDING (constrained to evidence):

1. "All evaluated reduced-order preservation models failed under the defined survival criteria."

2. "The organizational structure was not preserved under any tested single, pairwise, triple, or quadruple feature-preservation model."

3. "The results support an irreducible five-factor dependency under the evaluated intervention framework."

4. "Across 30+ tested feature-preservation model combinations, no reduced-order model maintained eigenvalue/spectral-gap structure within the 15% survival threshold."

NOT APPROVED (exceeds evidence):
- "universal"
- "fundamental law"  
- "proof"
- "impossible"
- "absolute irreducibility"
- "definitively proven"
- "100% certainty"

SAFE BOUNDARY: The claim is constrained to the tested intervention space only.
