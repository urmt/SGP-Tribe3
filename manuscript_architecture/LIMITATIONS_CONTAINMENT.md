# LIMITATIONS & CLAIM CONTAINMENT

## 1. Random Projection Limitations
The PCA-informed structured projection may prioritize variance over structural detail.

## 2. Semantic Mapping Limitations
Text embeddings are based on tinyLLaMA; results may vary with larger language models.

## 3. Single-Subject/System Limitations
Analyses are largely conducted on simulated or model-derived data (TRIBE/Hierarchical/Sparse). Biological ground truth is predicted, not observed.

## 4. Estimator Limitations
D(k) using Participation Ratio assumes Euclidean metric properties, which may not capture non-linear topological features.

## 5. Curvature-Definition Limitations
Curvature estimation using k-nearest neighbors is approximate and sensitive to local density.

## 6. Biological Interpretation Uncertainty
The Sentient Potential functional ($\chi$) is a theoretical construct and requires direct empirical validation.
