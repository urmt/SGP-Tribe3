STABILITY STATISTICS INTERPRETATION
==================================================

Question: Is stability metric realistic?

Reported '1.000' stability was based on single trial.
With 50 trials:

Subsample 50%: mean=0.999 ± 0.000
Subsample 75%: mean=1.000 ± 0.000
Noise σ=0.1: mean=0.997 ± 0.002
Noise σ=0.2: mean=0.991 ± 0.008
Noise σ=0.5: mean=0.958 ± 0.031

CONCLUSION: Stability remains HIGH but with real variance.
The 1.000 value was an artifact of single-trial estimation.
